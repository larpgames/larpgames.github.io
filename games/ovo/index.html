ABOUT OVO

OvO is a fast-paced platformer where you use parkour and free-running skills to complete each obstacle course. You can slide, jump off walls, and perform a ground pound to navigate through increasingly tricky levels.

The controls are simple but mastering the movement — especially chaining dives into slides and wall jumps — is what separates speedrunners from casual players.

PRO TIPS
  • Jump then immediately slide off a ledge to keep your momentum — this is the core speed trick.
  • Wall-jump by pressing jump right as you touch a wall. You can chain multiple walls in a row.
  • The ground-pound (down while airborne) lets you smash through certain floors.
  • Don't rush early levels — learn the movement system before trying to speedrun.
  • Avoid the black spikes — one hit sends you back to the last checkpoint.
